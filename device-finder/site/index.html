ESPclock Device Finder

Find an ESPclock by DEVICE_ID, open its control panel, and generate a QR deep link.

Warning: This page is loaded via HTTPS. Browsers often block discovery requests to local http:// devices (mixed-content restriction).

Setup-mode deep-link support is possible: when connected to the device AP ESPclock-<DEVICE_ID>, open http://192.168.4.1/.
